Rules of Play

  1. There are 2 players.
  2. Press the Play button to start.
  3. Your top 10 songs each have points allocated and accumulate.
  4. Press the Save & Switch button to save your score and switch players.
  5. Look out for the JOKER who will reset your score to 100 and ruin your 1980's flashbacks. She will also switch to the other player.
  6. You must Save & Switch to save your score.
  7. The first player to reach 0 wins the game.
  8. GAME OVER, THANKS FOR PLAYING!
